Welcome to my website.

Hello! My name is Denny, and I am a second-year Computer Science student at the University of Texas at Austin. Born in Keller, Tx, I have been a native Texan my entire life. Moving to Austin for UT's excellent Computer Science program has been very exciting and worthwhile.

I started programming 4 years ago in high school. Since then, my passion for Computer Science has only grown.

Goals for this semester

Jumping into the world of Computer Science can be overwhelming at first. There are so many languages I have to learn and gain experience with. I am working every day to gain experience in other languages and systems, such as C, Linux, and Python.

Last summer I was fortunate enough to work for Abbott Diagnostics as an Associate Scientist. As a member of the software verification team, I gained valuable corporate experience while working in an FDA-regulated professional lab. Next summer I hope to work for a company that will expand my knowledge of Computer Science and help develop my career.

Hobbies
