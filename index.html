Welcome to my website.

Hello! My name is Denny, and I am a first-year Computer Science student at the University of Texas at Austin. Born in Keller, Tx, I have been a native Texan my entire life. Moving to Austin for UT's excellent Computer Science program has been very exciting and worthwhile.

I started programming 3 years ago in high school. Since then, my passion for Computer Science has only grown.

Goals for this semester

Jumping into the world of Computer Science can be overwhelming at first. There are so many languages I have to learn and gain experience with. Right now, I consider myself only proficient with Java, but I am working every day to gain experience in other languages and systems, namely Linux and Python.

One big goal for me is to get an internship in Computer Science this summer. Doing so will expose me to all sorts of new concepts and experience that will be invaluable to me later on.
